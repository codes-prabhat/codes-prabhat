📖 THE NAME

In Sanskrit, Prabhat (प्रभात) means "dawn" — the first light that breaks through darkness. It's that quiet moment when the world resets, full of possibility. My parents named me after that feeling — a fresh start, every single day. And honestly? That's how I try to show up — curious, optimistic, ready to build something new.
